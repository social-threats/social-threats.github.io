Topics: The topics of the workshop include but are not limited to the following:

  • Advances in Natural Language Processing (NLP) for understanding online conversations, e.g., semantic parsing,
  • information retrieval and question answering
  • Dialogue/narrative understanding and generation for bots to counter adversaries
  • Detection of social threats, e.g., social engineering attacks, in online conversations
  • Automatic detection of actions and intentions of participants in online conversations, e.g., the implied “ask”
  • in the sentence Your vote here will result in eligibility for a $500 prize.
  • Natural language generation techniques to enable bot development for controlled, goal-directed and yet natural
  • sounding conversations with potential adversaries.
  • Persuasion techniques used in dialogue/narrative in social engineering contexts
  • Active and passive defense mechanisms used for development of conversational bots
  • Identification of attitudes that adversaries attempt to induce in targets for compliance
  • Techniques to induce attitudes in the adversaries, e.g., in counter attacks
  • Risk models for operating NLP bots with discretion and autonomy to converse with adversary
  • Social impact of social engineering attacks, persuasion techniques, etc. using language
  • Evaluation of the impact of different types of social engineering attacks
  • Evaluation of approaches to detecting and countering threats using language as a tool
